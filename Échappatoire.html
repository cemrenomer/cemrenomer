ÉCHAPPATOIRE

2019

>

Échappatoire beckons seekers of refuge into its depths, its very name evoking a sense of escapism. Conceived as a sanctuary from the harsh gaze of daylight, this immersive creation embraces the structural essence of a labyrinthine maze, where shadows dance and sunbeams are intentionally barred from entry.

Within the intricate pathways of this labyrinth, a hidden chamber awaits those with keen perception—a cryptic "Easteregg" room concealed from casual observation, reserved solely for the discerning eye. It stands as a testament to the thoughtful curation that permeate the installation's every detail, enticing explorers to embark on a journey of discovery and transcendence.

At its core, this enigmatic project delves deeply into the profound fear that encircles humanity; the fear of mortality, of fleeting existence, in all its myriad manifestations. It presents itself as a sanctuary, a place of solace where individuals can find respite from the omnipresent specter of death through the medium of numbers, embracing a numerical escape from this existential dread.

In essence, "Échappatoire" emerges as an embodiment of the eternal quest to outrun mortality's shadow. It invites contemplation on the transient nature of life, the boundaries that confine our mortal selves, and the yearning for a haven where fears can be cast aside. Through its labyrinthine design, it encourages introspection and the gradual unraveling of hidden truths, leading individuals along a winding path of self-discovery and reflection.

As visitors venture deeper into the recesses of this confusing "Maze", they are enticed to confront their own mortality, their fears entwined with the physicality of the space. Yet, within this encounter lies the potential for liberation—a delicate interplay of shadows, light, and hidden treasures that encourage the transcendence of mortal concerns.

Thus, "Échappatoire" transcends its physical boundaries to become a metaphorical gateway, offering an intricate and multisensory exploration of the human condition. It is an artistic tapestry that weaves together the complexity of our fears, our aspirations, and our eternal longing for immortality, inviting us to find solace, enlightenment, and the profound connection that lies beyond the veil of mortality.
