About the Artist

Cemre Nomer is a Turkish multidisciplinary artist. In 2021, she recieved her contemporary arts diploma from édhéa - École de Design et Haute École d’Art du Valais.

Cemre now lives and works in northern Switzerland and curates “Espace Formaline” in Suhr (Kanton Aargau). Her practice is by default versatile and socially engaged. Themes such as death, virtuality, artificial intelligence and identity creates the base of her work.
