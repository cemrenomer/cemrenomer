CHAOTIC

2020

Disarray and dissonance intertwine as parallel descriptors in this linguistic realm. Whenever the door in the video starts moving, neither indications nor anticipations emerge to guide the course of events. Chaos, a colossal concept, has a myriad of subordinate facets. A select few of these facets are integrated fabric of this undertaking, conveyed through the resolute intonations of the narrator's voice.
